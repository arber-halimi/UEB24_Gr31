Fossils from the Dawn of Time

Step into the ancient past and uncover the secrets of life from millions of years ago in Fossils from the Dawn of Time. This captivating exhibition invites you to explore the incredible story of Earth’s early inhabitants, told through the preserved remains of plants, animals, and ecosystems that shaped our world. Begin with the earliest traces of life, from microscopic organisms preserved in ancient rock to stromatolites, the living fossils that provide a window into Earth’s first ecosystems. Marvel at the fossils of the Cambrian Explosion, a time of unprecedented biodiversity when complex life forms first appeared in the oceans. Journey through the Paleozoic Era, where giant sea scorpions, trilobites, and the first land-dwelling creatures emerged. Witness the evolution of vertebrates and explore fossils from the Carboniferous period, when vast forests of towering ferns and ancient amphibians dominated the landscape. Step into the Mesozoic Era, the age of dinosaurs, and encounter fossils of the mighty creatures that ruled the land, sea, and sky. See fossilized remains of iconic species like Triceratops and Pterosaurs, and learn how their world changed with the cataclysmic events that marked the end of their reign. Finally, explore the Cenozoic Era, the rise of mammals, and the evolution of early humans. From the towering mammoths to the saber-toothed cats, this era brings you closer to understanding how life adapted and thrived after the extinction of the dinosaurs. Throughout the exhibition, discover how fossils are uncovered, studied, and preserved by paleontologists. Hands-on displays and interactive elements offer insights into the science of fossilization and the tools used to unlock ancient mysteries. Fossils from the Dawn of Time is more than an exhibition—it’s a journey through the history of life itself, offering a deeper understanding of our planet’s remarkable past and the forces that continue to shape its future.
